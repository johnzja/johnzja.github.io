Biography
======
Jieao Zhu is a Ph.D. candidate in Electronic Engineering of Tsinghua University from September 2021 under the supervision of Professor Linglong Dai. He worked as a visiting student at National University of Singapore (NUS) under the supervision of Professor Vincent Y. F. Tan from Feburary 2024 to June 2024, studying finite-blocklength information theory. His current research is mainly focused on advanced RIS technologies (fabrication and demo), electromagnetic information theory (EIT), MIMO/RIS signal processing, and Rydberg atomic receivers.
